About Candle Décor

Candle Décor was born from a passion for creating warm, elegant spaces. Every candle is crafted with care, using natural wax and delicate fragrances that bring harmony, comfort and beauty to your home.

Each Candle Décor piece is hand-poured in small batches using eco-friendly soy wax and carefully selected fragrances. We believe that a candle is more than a decoration – it is a feeling. Our mission is to turn everyday moments into calm, cozy experiences filled with warmth, balance and subtle luxury.
